lettering.

This campaign project was to create two original marketing campaigns to raise money and awareness for the Niagara Peninsula Conservation Foundation. This project involved coding the websites, creating posters, weekly social media posts on Facebook and Twitter and using Mail Chimp for email newsletters. Engagement was recorded using Google Analytics and various social media analytics. The most challenging part of this was maintaining a consistant brand identity across the many outputs. The final outcome is two sucessful marketing campaigns which raised $125 each.
