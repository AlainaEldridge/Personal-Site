Roomies is a roommate survival kit designed to help postsecondary roommates adjust to living together. It is based on three personality profiles determined when students complete an online quiz at the time of college registration. Quinn is for extroverts who are outgoing and talkative, Eliott is for those who are social but value their alone time, and Taylor is for introverts who love their alone time and are quiet. Students are matched according to their profile and recieve the corresponding kit. Kits contain items such as: a roommate contract, a rules and chores poster, recipe cards, and branded freebies such as a hat and tote bag.
